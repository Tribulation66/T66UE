## Translation Rules

- Prefer dark stone, slate, bronze, and desaturated gold over flat neon accents.
- Frames should read as layered metal or carved stone, not soft modern cards.
- Use uppercase headings with ceremonial weight; keep body text smaller and more compact.
- Inventory, idol, and ability slots should feel inset and bordered, never flat.
- Modal overlays should read as heavy in-world panels over a dark scrim.
- When unsure between "clean" and "ornate", bias toward ornate as long as readability holds.
